Jakka Pranav Swaroop Naidu

Founder and CEO

Jakka Pranav Naidu, the Founder and CEO of MZ INNOVATE HUB Pvt. Ltd., leads the executive team with a vision to drive innovation in the digital industry. At just 28 years old, he is a dynamic leader committed to transforming the industry. Alongside his childhood friend, he founded MZ INNOVATE HUB Pvt. Ltd. in 2015. With an impressive track record in the digital space, he established the company under the tagline, "Remarkably Delivers the Best."

Despite his young age, Jakka possesses extensive expertise in Sales, Digital Marketing, Marketing Management, Market Planning, and Market Strategy. His commitment to uniqueness in every endeavor has positioned him as the youngest and most successful entrepreneur at MZ INNOVATE HUB Pvt. Ltd. His deep understanding of business development and consumer behavior has been instrumental in shaping the Scintillate System over the years.

In just two years since founding MZ INNOVATE HUB Pvt. Ltd., Jakka has earned multiple prestigious awards and recognitions, including "Young Entrepreneur of India" and "Top 500 CEOs of India." His relentless drive for excellence inspires his team to consistently deliver high-quality results.

As a leader, Jakka believes in collaboration and considers himself an integral part of the team. He is always ready to step in whenever necessary to support and drive success.

Jakka often shares his perspective on leadership, saying, “I have met many business leaders and professionals with great ideas and passion. However, great plans require execution, and choosing the right path can be challenging. It’s easy to get stuck in tricky situations, but the key is patience and maximizing what you can achieve within given limitations. Once you understand this, success is yours.”

Overall, Jakka Pranav Naidu is an energetic, highly motivated team player and a visionary leader who continues to shape the future of MZ INNOVATE HUB Pvt. Ltd.
